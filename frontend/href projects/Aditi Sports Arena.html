Basketball Rules

  • Each team has 5 players on the court at a time.
  • The game consists of 4 quarters of 10 minutes each.
  • A field goal is worth 2 points; 3 points if shot beyond the arc.
  • Free throws are worth 1 point each.
  • No running with the ball ‚Äî you must dribble while moving.
  • 24-second shot clock for each possession.
  • Personal fouls result in free throws after 5 team fouls per quarter.
  • The team with the most points at the end wins.
