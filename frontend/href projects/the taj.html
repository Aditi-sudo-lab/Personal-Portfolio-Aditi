Booking our Hotel

ELEGANCE BEYOND COMPARE

Our boomerang-shaped luxury hotel in Delhi is spread over six acres of verdant greenery and balances modern interiors with regional art to create a luxurious ambience. With sustainability top of mind, we also ensure you breathe the purest air with a special electronic air filtration system. Step into our grand lobby and be enveloped almost immediately by an air of refinement, where sparkling chandeliers, gilded stairways, carpeted floors and fresh blooms welcome you. The peacock-themed elevators bring you to rooms and suites that offer stunning views of the ridge forest and the Diplomatic Enclave.

Highlights

A haven of indulgent comfort in the centre of the city, Taj Palace, New Delhi is where business and leisure travellers alike, find a regal retreat. With delicious dining options, versatile venues and opulent rooms, we set the hallmark for our commitment to your comfort.
